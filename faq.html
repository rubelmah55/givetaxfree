How It Works

Home How It Works

Givetaxfree.org is a non-profit crowdfunding site. How does crowd fundraising work? Well, all your queries are answered here!

The recipient’s campaign is directly connected with a secure third party credit card processing company (Stripe) that processes the donations received. Givetaxfree.org does not have access to the credit card information of people who make donations.
